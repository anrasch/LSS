Shadows of Revolution

Shadows of Revolution is a gripping dystopian novel that immerses the reader in the dark world of the city of Ecliptas. In a society controlled by megacorporations, Maya and Gabe fight against oppression and for freedom. The protagonist, Maya, shaped by her unique heritage and deep determination, is at the center of this fight. Together with her group of rebels, she tries to bring the truth about the rulers' machinations to light and mobilize the citizens of Ecliptas. The plot is filled with tension, secrets, and moral dilemmas that captivate the reader. The strength of the book lies not only in its suspenseful storyline but also in its profound characters and the complex relationships that connect them.
