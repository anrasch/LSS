Nova Polis - Stories of the Future

The Night of the Ascension

In the Neon District of Nova Polis, musician and street performer Theo Marlowe dreams of making it big. One evening, he is discovered by Jasper Blake, a renowned music producer, who offers him the chance to record demos. At the Hyperion Club, Jade Rivera offers him an exclusive show, further advancing his career.

Work is currently underway to expand the story and add new chapters.
